Privacy Policy

1. Purpose

This policy outlines the rules and guidelines for purchasing, returns, exchanges, and customer service at ShoeMaker. It ensures a smooth shopping experience while maintaining fairness and transparency for both customers and the store.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all customers, employees, and business partners who interact with ShoeMaker. It covers online and in-store purchases, shipping, returns, exchanges, and customer support.

3. Roles and Responsibilities

Store Manager: Oversees policy enforcement and customer issue resolution.

Customers: Must adhere to store policies when making purchases, returns, or exchanges.

4. Policy Guidelines

4.1 Ordering & Payment

Orders can be placed online through our website or in-store.

We accept payments via VN pay.

Orders must be paid in full before shipping or pickup.

Discounts and promotions cannot be combined unless stated otherwise.

4.2 Shipping & Delivery

Orders are processed within 1 business days after payment confirmation.

Delivery times vary based on location but typically range from 3-4 days.

Customers will receive tracking details via email/SMS once the order is shipped.

We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party couriers.

4.3 Returns & Exchanges

Returns are accepted within 1 day of purchase if the product is unused, undamaged, and in original packaging.

Refunds are issued in the original payment method within 1 business days after approval.

Exchanges are allowed for sizing issues, but the customer must cover shipping costs.

Custom or final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.

4.4 Warranty & Defective Products

We offer a 1 day warranty against manufacturing defects.

If the product is defective, customers must provide proof of purchase and clear images of the defect.

Replacement or refund decisions will be made upon inspection.

4.5 Customer Conduct & Store Rights

Customers must treat staff with respect; abusive behavior will not be tolerated.

We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone violating store policies.

Fraudulent activities (e.g., chargebacks, fake returns) will be reported to authorities.

4.6 Contact & Support

For any issues, customers can reach us via facebook during business hours.

Our support team will respond within 5 business hours.
